Time Management

Why Does this Page exist?

Since I began my college career at Cal Poly, I have felt I had a duty to work part-time while attending school to ease my own and my family’s financial burden from student loans. Sadly, I struggled with balancing time for school and work.

This is my attempt to fix that problem.

I use Timely to log my lecture and study hours.

I figured making my timesheets public would reinforce the habit.

While the complex psychology of habit formation can be expressed simply, in practice it is a difficult but enjoyable challenge.
